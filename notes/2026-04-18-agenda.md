## Agenda (60 minutes)

### 14:00–14:05 – Welcome (5 min)

- Moderator welcomes participants
- Reminder of the [Code of Conduct](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/community-code-of-conduct/)
- Accessibility check: any needs we should be aware of?

### 14:05–14:15 – Introductions & Ice Breaker (10 min)

Each participant shares briefly (1–2 min):

- **Name & location**
- **Role in the WordPress ecosystem**
- **Contribution story:** How did your WordPress contribution journey start?

### 14:15–14:25 – Topic Overview (10 min)

- Background: how did the Office Hours idea come about?
- Summary of the [proposal](https://make.wordpress.org/training/2026/02/03/proposal-monthly-office-hour-meeting-for-make-wordpress-training/) and community feedback
- What do we hope to decide or achieve today?

### 14:25–14:50 – Main Discussion (25 min)

**Topic:** Shaping the Office Hours format together

Discussion questions:

1. Does the proposed 60-minute structure work for everyone? What would you change?
2. What should be recurring agenda items in every meeting?
3. How do we make new contributors feel welcome and leave with a concrete task?
4. What does success look like after the 3-meeting pilot phase?
5. Should we manage the meeting organisation (agendas, notes, action items) in GitHub?
6. How do we track open and completed action items from each meeting?

### 14:50–15:00 – Next Steps, Action Items & Wrap-Up (10 min)

- What decisions did we make today?
- Who takes which task? (Name + target date)
- When is the next meeting? Who moderates?
- Open questions
- Thank you to all participants
- Notes will be published on the Make WordPress Training blog within ~48h
- Post Slack closing tag: `</office hour meeting>`
