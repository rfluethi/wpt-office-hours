# Contributing to Make WordPress Training Office Hours

Welcome! There are several ways you can contribute to the Office Hours initiative.

## Propose a Topic

The easiest way to contribute is to suggest a topic for a future meeting.

1. Go to [Issues → New Issue](../../issues/new?template=topic-proposal.md)
2. Select the **Topic Proposal** template
3. Fill in the details and submit

Your proposal will appear on the [Kanban Board](../../projects) in the **Ideas** column. The team will review it and move it to **Planned** when it's scheduled for an upcoming meeting.

## Review Meeting Notes

After each meeting, a draft of the notes is published here in [`/notes`](notes/) before being posted on the Make WordPress Training blog.

To suggest a correction or addition:
- Open the file in `/notes/`
- Comment directly on the file or open an Issue referencing it
- You have approximately **48 hours** after the meeting to contribute

Once the notes are approved, they'll be published on [make.wordpress.org/training](https://make.wordpress.org/training/).

## Moderate a Meeting

We rotate moderators to share facilitation responsibility and help people build confidence. If you're interested:

- No experience required – templates and checklists are provided
- A co-moderator will support you
- You moderate at most once every 3 months
- You can always decline without explanation

Express your interest in the `#training` Slack channel or by commenting on an upcoming meeting issue.

## Kanban Board Columns

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Ideas** | Proposed topics, open for community input |
| **Planned** | Accepted for an upcoming meeting |
| **Done** | Discussed – linked to published meeting notes |

## Labels

| Label | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `topic-proposal` | A suggested topic for a future meeting |
| `meta` | Discussion about the Office Hours format itself |
| `help-wanted` | Looking for someone to take on a task |
| `timezone-APAC` | Relevant for an APAC-friendly meeting time |
| `timezone-Americas` | Relevant for an Americas-friendly meeting time |
