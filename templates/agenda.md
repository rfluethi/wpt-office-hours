## Agenda (60 minutes)

### 00:00–05:00 – Welcome & Introductions (5 min)

- Moderator welcomes participants
- Quick introductions: name, location, role in the WordPress ecosystem
- Reminder of the Code of Conduct
- Accessibility check: any needs we should be aware of?

### 05:00–15:00 – Topic Overview (10 min)

- Brief introduction to the meeting topic
- Context and background
- What do we hope to decide or achieve today?

### 15:00–45:00 – Main Discussion (30 min)

**Topic:** *[TOPIC TITLE]*

2.
3.

### 45:00–55:00 – Next Steps & Action Items (10 min)

- What decisions did we make today?
- Who takes which task? (Name + target date)
- When is the next meeting? Who moderates?

### 55:00–60:00 – Open Q&A & Wrap-Up (5 min)

- Open questions
- Thank you to all participants
- Notes will be published on the Make WordPress Training blog within ~48h
- Post Slack closing tag: `</office hour meeting>`

---

## Before the Meeting (Moderator Checklist)

- [ ] Set up and test Zoom link
- [ ] Create and share calendar invite
- [ ] Publish announcement post on make.wordpress.org/training
- [ ] Send reminder in Slack `#training` (1 week + 24h before)
- [ ] Share this agenda and the [notes template](../templates/meeting-notes.md) with the note-taker
- [ ] Link this agenda file in the announcement post at least 48h before
- [ ] At meeting start: post in Slack `<office hour meeting>` + Zoom link (logs time for [Five for the Future](https://make.wordpress.org/five-for-the-future/))

---

## After the Meeting (Checklist)

- [ ] Note-taker creates `YYYY-MM-DD-meeting.md` in `/notes/` (use [meeting-notes template](../templates/meeting-notes.md))
- [ ] Participants have ~48h to suggest corrections via GitHub comments
- [ ] Publish approved notes as blog post on make.wordpress.org/training
- [ ] Update the notes file with the blog post link
- [ ] Share action items in Slack `#training`
- [ ] Announce next meeting date and moderator
- [ ] Collect feedback (Slack thread or short form)

---

## Cancellation Process

If the meeting needs to be cancelled:

- Notify participants at least **48 hours** in advance (earlier if possible)
- Post cancellation notice in Slack `#training`
- If the co-moderator is available, they may take over instead
- Reschedule within the same month if possible, otherwise note in the next Tuesday meeting
