# Handbook

This directory contains working documents for integrating the Office Hours format into the [Make WordPress Training Handbook](https://make.wordpress.org/training/handbook/).

The documents are drafts intended for review and discussion within the Training Team before being submitted as handbook contributions. They are maintained here during the pilot phase of Office Hours and will be proposed for inclusion in the official handbook after the pilot is evaluated.

---

## Documents

### [Organizing Office Hours Meetings](Organizing%20Office%20Hours%20Meetings.md)

**Purpose:** A complete process guide for anyone organizing, moderating, or taking notes at an Office Hours meeting.

**Intended destination:** A new handbook page under [Training Team How-To Guides](https://make.wordpress.org/training/handbook/training-team-how-to-guides/), titled *Organizing Office Hours Meetings*.

**Covers:**
- Planning the meeting (scheduling, agenda creation, Slack announcement)
- Running the meeting (Slack tags, meeting structure, after the call)
- Creating GitHub issues for action items
- Publishing the meeting notes (draft in the agenda issue, 48h correction window, blog post publication)
- Moderation and note-taking rotation guidelines
- Contingency procedures (low attendance, technical issues, Code of Conduct)

## Status

These documents are working drafts. They reflect the process as established during the Office Hours pilot phase (April–June 2026).

After the pilot evaluation, the plan is to:
1. Propose *Organizing Office Hours Meetings* as a new handbook page via the standard handbook contribution process.
2. Submit the proposed changes to existing pages for review by Team Reps and Admins.
3. Transfer this repository into the official Training Team structures.
